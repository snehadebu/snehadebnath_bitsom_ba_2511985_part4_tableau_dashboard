# Executive Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary

The dashboard provides a consolidated view of retail business performance by combining sales, profitability, customer behavior, shipping performance, discount analysis, and return trends into one executive dashboard.

## What is Performing Well

- The South region generates the highest profit.
- Technology products contribute the largest share of profit.
- Home Office customers contribute strong sales performance.
- Customer ratings remain consistently high.

## Underperforming Areas

- Furniture experiences the highest return levels.
- Standard shipping requires the longest delivery time.
- Some product categories generate lower profitability.

## Business Risks

- Increasing discounts reduce overall profitability.
- High return rates can impact customer satisfaction.
- Longer delivery times may negatively affect customer experience.

## Business Opportunities

- Expand investment in high-performing regions.
- Increase focus on Technology products.
- Improve inventory planning.
- Promote faster shipping options.
- Optimize discount strategies.

## Recommended Business Actions

- Continue supporting profitable regions.
- Improve logistics for Standard shipping.
- Monitor return-heavy product categories.
- Reduce unnecessary discounts.
- Improve customer experience through faster delivery.

## Limitations

The dashboard reflects only the available historical dataset. External market conditions and seasonal effects are not included.

## Suggested Next Analysis

Future analysis should include:

- Customer lifetime value
- Marketing campaign performance
- Forecasting future sales
- Inventory optimization
