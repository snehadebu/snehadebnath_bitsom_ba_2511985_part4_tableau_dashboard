# Business Insights

## Insight 1 – Sales Trend

Observation:
Monthly sales fluctuate throughout the year.

Evidence:
The Monthly Sales Trend chart shows noticeable monthly variation.

Business Interpretation:
Sales are influenced by seasonality and purchasing behavior.

Recommendation:
Plan promotions during lower-performing months.

---

## Insight 2 – Regional Performance

Observation:
South region generates the highest profit.

Evidence:
Regional Performance chart.

Business Interpretation:
The South region performs better than other regions.

Recommendation:
Continue investment in South while improving weaker regions.

---

## Insight 3 – Category Profitability

Observation:

Evidence:
Category Profitability chart.

Technology products provide higher business value.
