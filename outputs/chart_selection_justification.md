# Chart Selection Justification

## Monthly Sales Trend

Question Answered:
How do sales change over time?

Chart Used:
Line Chart

Reason:
A line chart clearly shows monthly sales trends.

Design Principle:
Chronological ordering.

Mistake Avoided:
Avoided pie charts for time-series data.

---

## Regional Performance

Question Answered:
Which region performs best?

Chart Used:
Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason:
Easy comparison between regions.

Design Principle:
Sorted bars improve readability.

Mistake Avoided:
Avoided unnecessary 3D charts.

---

## Category Profitability

Question Answered:
Which product categories generate the highest profit?

Chart Used:
Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason:
Allows comparison across categories and sub-categories.

Design Principle:
Color used for category distinction.

Mistake Avoided:
Avoided cluttered stacked charts.

---

## Customer Segment

Question Answered:
Which customer segment contributes the most sales?

Chart Used:
Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason:
Simple comparison across segments.

Design Principle:
Consistent color scheme.

Mistake Avoided:

## Shipping Performance

Question Answered:
Which shipping mode takes the longest delivery time?

Chart Used:
Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason:
Easy comparison of average delivery days.

Design Principle:
Clear axis labels.

Mistake Avoided:
Avoided misleading scales.

---

## Discount vs Profit

Question Answered:
How does discount affect profitability?

Chart Used:
Scatter Plot

Reason:
Shows relationship between discount and profit.

Design Principle:
Trend line highlights overall relationship.

Mistake Avoided:
Avoided bar charts for correlation analysis.

---

## Return Analysis

Question Answered:
Which categories experience the highest returns?

Chart Used:
Stacked Bar Chart

Reason:
Displays return counts by category.

Design Principle:
Color distinguishes regions.

Mistake Avoided:
Avoided overly complex visualizations.
